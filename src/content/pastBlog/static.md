---
title: "Static"
date: 2015-12-17
---

I have hit a new point in my never ending spiral towards madness. For years when I had a depressive week it felt like a knife slowly carving out my heart. It was a dull aching pain that pulsed slowly. Sometimes fading into the back ground to be forgotten but always coming back. It was at these moments highs and lows that I was mostly active in life. Because I still felt something, I still had internal pain that in some odd way kept me going.

These last few months though, something new has happened. The best way I can describe it is static. Where my life before was hills and valleys this is like a flat path. But fill the path with sound proof foam and roll me up into it. No longer do I feel much of anything any more. I still feel the need to use the bathroom, eat and sleep. Everything else is just a low hiss in the background.

For a while I thought it was just a valley that I would come out of, but as time moves forward nothing changes. In some ways its a good thing. I feel less unstable, more sure of my sanity. In other ways it is detrimental. Things that I hold valuable and things that bring me joy look gray. Nothing holds my interest or thought anymore. Because of that i have become even less active in my own life. I have withdrawn more.

It makes me so worried that I will never come out of it. Spending time with friends something that in the past always could snap me out of anything. Now it is just a waiting game. Just me waiting till the obligation to smile, laugh and act like a functional human being is over. I understand how awful that is to say. I also wish I knew how to take the mask off and show how I truly feel.

Something about my personality or the way I was raised caused me to be entertaining and a people person. I have no clue how to stop it. If I just sit around quietly people just ask me whats wrong. Of course something is wrong but no one wants to hear anyones issues. My mind knows that if I am a bummer all the time I will loose everyone. So it puts on a show. The only time I don’t feel tension is when I am alone. When with people I’m burning special fuel that I don’t have much off. Its why I often plan days of nothing after days of seeing friends. To rest and recharge, to get over the fact that i cant feel for them the way i used too.

From what I have read this is the last stage. Nothing much more happens after this. You either find help or you stay in the static. I dont know how long I can stay here. Right now there is a odd comforting nature about it. For months I have needed to call one number to set up a doctors appointment. For months I have done nothing. Why cant I just call? Have I grown accustom to being nothing. Am I scared of how much a feeling mind will hate what I have become? Change is hard. Thats what it is. I will call. I have to call.

-Tom
