---
title: "Deterioration"
date: 2015-08-05
---

I am taking this time to explain why i am a failure. This is not necessarily a bad thing. Often you will hear influential people explain that failures make us who we are. Well in that case at some point in time, i will be super great. Today though, not great. Abysmally bad. This also is not happening all the sudden. Its taken years. In fact i don’t even know if i ever was successful. I have a hard time ever thinking i was.

It all started when i was a kid and things came ever so slightly to easily for me. It made me think i could coast through life without trying and be successful. As we all know, that is not how the real world works. When i started home schooling i came to a standstill and something that should have taken me a year or two tops took three or four. Only finishing because i felt bad about how much money my mom had spent to have me home school.

When i started home schooling i got a part time job. This job gave me close to six hundred dollars a month. For fifteen year old me that was a kings wealth. So on my off days and weekends i would take my car to the city and goof off. I did nothing productive with my life. Like learning a trade or useful skill. I thought i could coast, that it would all work out. Things come easy to me, remember. I was so incredibly wrong.

I've moved away from my parents house twice. Coming back both times because i couldn’t handle life. I always had excuses for moving home the first time. Looking back on it now with fresh eyes i can see that it was my failure to cope with life. The second time i moved home, that i knew exactly what was happening.

I struggle to know when im being terrible to my self because of my brains chemical imbalances and when i am just terrible at life naturally. This is because my brain is almost constantly telling me that i am a worthless waist of space that shouldn’t exist. Now i know that everyone gets this way sometimes. So i thought it was completely normal. It turns out that it should be a brief passing thought. Not a pervasive idea that shapes everything in your world view. This type of thought pattern also often leads to anxity for witch i also thought was completly normal. Turns out, not the case. (If you get bored and want to read about major depressive disorder go

here!

)

I don’t want to use it as a crutch though. I have traits that don’t work. I am quite lazy. I give up incredibly fast. I bore easily. I can be judgmental and rude. These are things that most people find ways to work through. I too date have none of them handled. They are worsened by my brains self loathing. Most days i set up one task for me to do. Do laundry. Wash dishes. Clean Bathroom. Mail a letter. Buy groceries. These are things that most of the world accomplishes in one day easily. They seem like giant mountains to me. Often even though its just one simple task i fail to do anything. Encased in sloth like anxiety and self doubt.

I live in the sentience “whats the point.” When everything looks like a cliff face sheering off into a black pit of nothingness then having fresh clothes or washed dishes seems like, well pointless. So most of my days i spend distracting my self. Reading, listening and watching my self into a coma. This is the only time that my brain settles down for a bit. It stays quiet from the relentless beat down.

So when the only time you feel slightly ok is when you are consuming content you fall into a routine. My daily tasks get ignored. Work feels like a endless drag that can only be fixed by getting home and not thinking any more. I am self medicating my self by ignoring my problems. I am wasting my life in this process. No marriageable woman would ever look at me and see a provider or a useful companion. I don’t blame them. That last bit is just a dig at my self. Ignore it. Back to the point at hand.

I am a failure.
