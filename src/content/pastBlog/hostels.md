title: "Hostels"
date: 2015-01-31
---

The drive was about a hour long. Alonzo, Asa and Rachel where in the rental car. Kevin and I in his car. He drives a old honda shuttle, a car that by all merits should be dead but just keeps going. We where driving from Fairfield to San Francisco. Kevin had just graduated BSSB and we all took a few extra days to explore San Francisco with him.

We had all been planing this to be as cheep as posible. So we chose to stay in a hostel. Something only kevin and Alonzo had done before. So i wasent shure what to expect when we pulled up. I think it was a old army base. When we checked in there was some issue with the rooming. They like to keep the genders separated. We didn’t want Rachel off on her own without Asa so we all got put in big room together.

The room just had single bunk beds lining the walls with storage lockers at the bottom for your stuff. I loved this place. It was like a big family feeling. Everyone that was staying there was on a big adventure. There was a big common room, a huge kitchen and a place for every one to watch movies. Sadly it was Halloween that night so the festivities where in full swing.

My most vivid memory of the hostel was sitting in the common room late into the night talking and watching videos on our ipads. Every night Alonzo would come and sit with us and promptly fall asleep on the couch. Rachel found a video of kevin making a funny sound from a day back in Texas. It was the funniest thing in that moment. We played it over and over.

Over all the experience was a great one. I await the days when we wont have to fear random crazy people. That the since of family will be felt every where. That every one can go and have adventures, stay wherever and meet people.

Every one has a story to tell or dreams to live.
