New month, new page. This one's a woodland gathering — fairy lanterns strung
through the roots, a whole party happening underground while one kid just
sits up top, unimpressed by the whole thing. If I'm tagging myself, that's
the one.

Flipping this calendar over is turning into one of my favorite small
monthly rituals — see who's living their best life this time, get back to
it.
