Stood still for probably twenty minutes while a few hundred thousand bats
poured out of the ground at dusk and just kept coming. The sound alone is
worth the trip — that much wingbeat at once turns into a kind of static.

Filed under: things that make Texas weirder and better than people give it
credit for.
