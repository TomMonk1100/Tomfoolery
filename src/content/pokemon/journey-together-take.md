---
title: "Journey Together gets too much hate"
---
Gonna say it: Journey Together is a lovely set and it doesn't deserve the
pile-on it gets in collector circles. The trainer art alone earns it a
spot on the shelf, and there's more than one pull in there that's grown on
me every time I look at it.

Not every set has to be a chase-card bloodbath to be worth busting.
